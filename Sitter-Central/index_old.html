Leaving your child with a complete stranger is dangerous. Let us reassure you.

One of the hardest parts about hiring babysitters is trust. How can you trust a complete stranger to watch over your pride and joy? The solution to this dilemma is our interactive learning platform for sitters. Lessons will be scaffolded specifically for pet sitting and babysitting. At the end of the course the sitter will receive a badge that is displayed on their profile letting parents know that they are approved. Paying for courses will be a source of monetization.
